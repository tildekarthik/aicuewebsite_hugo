Vision

Enable decision making with data science to ensure business results and bring easy to use products to all front liners

Founded in 2020, we work across the entire manufacturing value chain—from R&D and operations to sales and support——delivering measurable business results for OEMs, Tier 1 and Tier 2 automotive and heavy engineering/manufacturing customers, as well as global supply chain and logistics companies. Our AI-powered products make advanced analytics and automation accessible and easy to use, helping organizations improve productivity, safety,and quality and reduce costs.

With a strong commitment to customer value, fairness, and sustainability, AICUE Data Lab combines deep domain expertise with innovative technology to provide proven, scalable solutions such as visual inspection AI, real-time safety, productivity monitoring, and dealership analytics. Our mission is to democratize AI for manufacturing, ensuring that every frontline worker and decision-maker can benefit from actionable insights and smarter processes

Core Values

  1. Focus on Value to Customer, not revenue or margins
  2. Fairness, Sincerity and Integrity before growth and profits.
  3. Develop and Adopt a way of management to Assure Quality - The AICUE way
  4. Sustainability is more important than short term gains. Sustainability is across social, economic and environment and focussed on building organizational processes
